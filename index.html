Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

› › › › › › ›

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • ● PE100 grade material
  • ● Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion

Twin-screw extruders provide uniform melt flow and consistent wall thickness distribution throughout the full length of each pipe.

  • ● Controlled melt temperature
  • ● Uniform wall distribution

Controlled Cooling

Staged water-cooling baths maintain precise temperature gradients for stress-free pipe formation and dimensional stability.

  • ● Staged cooling zones
  • ● Stress-relief treatment

Precision Sizing

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure exact outer diameter with continuous monitoring ensuring every pipe meets ±0.1mm tolerance.

  • ● ±0.1mm diameter tolerance
  • ● Continuous monitoring

Quality Control

Multi-stage QC inspection tests dimensional accuracy, pressure ratings, and surface finish at every stage of production.

  • ● Pressure testing
  • ● Dimensional checks

Marking & Identification

Permanent ink-jet marking applies product code, diameter, pressure rating, and certification marks for full traceability.

  • ● Full traceability
  • ● Certification marks applied

Precision Cutting

Automated planetary cutters provide burr-free, square-end cuts to precise customer-specified lengths with zero material waste.

  • ● Burr-free cuts guaranteed
  • ● Custom lengths available

Packaging & Dispatch

Pipes are bundled, wrapped in UV-protective film, and dispatched with all required documentation and certifications included.

  • ● UV-protective wrapping
  • ● Full documentation included
